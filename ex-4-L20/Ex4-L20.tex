  \item Autômato finito determinístico que reconhece as cadeias da linguagem  $\mathcal{L}_\myling$:\\
    $M_\myling=\langle\Sigma=\{0,1\},S=\{s_0,s_1,s_2,s_3,s_4\},s_0,\delta,F=\{s_3,s_4\}\rangle$, com a função $\delta$ definida por:\\
    
    $$\begin{array}{|c|cc|}
     \hline
     \delta & 0   & 1\\
     \hline
        s_0 & s_1 & s_1\\
        s_1 & s_2 & s_1\\
        s_2 & s_3 & s_4\\
        s_3 & s_3 & s_4\\
        s_4 & s_2 & s_1\\
     \hline
    \end{array}$$
  
  \begin{center}
   \begin{tikzpicture}[

     \node[state,right of=s0]        (s1) {$s_1$};
     \node[state,right of=s1]        (s2) {$s_2$};
     \node[state,above of=s2, accept] (s3) {$s_3$};
     \node[state,below of=s2,accept] (s4) {$s_4$};

     \draw (s0) edge               node {0,1}   (s1)

           (s4) edge[bend left=20] node {0}   (s2)
                edge[bend left=20] node {1}   (s1)
                
   \end{tikzpicture}
  \end{center}
 \end{itemize}
